COVID-19

We follow CDC and WHO guidelines to secure your health and safety. We offer sanitizer, masks and gloves in all of our vehicles.

BNG role

We actively encourage sick drivers to stay home.
Provide employees with accurate information about COVID-19, its symptoms, and how it spreads.
Encourage the use of and provide assistance in acquiring hand sanitizer and disposable wipes and cleaning products so that commonly touched vehicle surfaces can be wiped down by drivers.
Develop policies and technology options that allow and prioritize contactless transactions that limit or eliminate close contact and the sharing of items such as pens and electronic signature pads between drivers and passengers.
Continuously measure driver’s temperature after/before rides.
All team members get tested weekly.

Chauffeur role

Avoid touching eyes, nose, or mouth.
Wear Mask & gloves.
Maintain social distance.
Wash hands regularly with soap and water for at least 20 seconds or use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer containing at least 60% alcohol. • Additional times on the job to clean hands include
Before and after work shifts
Before and after work breaks
After handling passengers’ personal belongings, if unavoidable
After putting on, touching, or removing cloth face coverings
Before wearing and after removing cold-weather gloves
Before and after pumping gas

Passenger role

Passengers to wear a cloth face covering and cover their mouth and nose.
Use tissues if cough or sneeze to be disposed by the passenger after exiting the vehicle.
Please use hand sanitizer immediately when entering/ exiting vehicle.
